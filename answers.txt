
Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: It's because it allows us to work on code without breaking the main code, and collaborate without overwriting other people's work on code. You can also back up your progress.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science is really useful in medicine as analyzing patient records and data trends in reactions to certain treatments can really give an answer as to how effective some medicine can be.


Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines two different branches that preserves the full branch history while rebasing moves commits onto another branch with no merge commit.
